Lowa is Celebrating 90 years of being a manufacturer of quality active footwear. They still manufacture 95% of their products, in their own factories in Europe. They continue to produce and design footwear, based on a tradition of quality - both in their product desing, and manufacturing. They are the only shoe manufacturer in the world with an ISO 9001 rating.

Lowa is a firm believer in footwear with Polyurethane midsoles. These shoes offer great benefits for walkers, hikers, workforce, taskforce, and hunters that desire better support, protection, and durability. This allows for the consumer to enjoy a better long term value and investment from their purchase.
