CW-X is a Leader in compression athletic wear, with over 10 years of distribution in the US market. They have over 50 worldwide patents designed to help support muscles, tendons, and joints on the active consumer’s body. The taping systems/support tape on their garments help support the bodies’ muscle groups and add control to the athletes oscillating muscles in active movements. Such as running and skiing. In addition, they build these quality polyester lycra garments with athletic compression standards. This helps support better blood flow, as well as quicker after workout recovery times.

Owned by Wacoal industries, a 2 billion dollar women’s underwear supplier, CW-X makes some of the most breathable, moisture-wicking, and supportive running bras in the world.

They are a leader in run specialty shops in the US, and our introducing new trail-worthy products for outdoor shops. This is a product line that continues to find new consumers every day. If you are active and haven’t tried CW-X, give us a call to get a test sample.
