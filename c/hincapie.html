Hincapie Apparel is a company started 8 years ago by Rich and George Hincapie to create a brand of Custom and Retail cycling apparel and accessories. They own and run a Columbia factory, which employs over 200 people. They continue to support their Hincapie development cycling team, to help create future pro cyclists from the US.

We can do shop jerseys at great prices, and have multiple options on styles and feature benefits.

We can offer you first rate retail cycling apparel styles, at mid-rate prices.

We can offer you a brand that is continuing to grow and participate and support the cycling community.
